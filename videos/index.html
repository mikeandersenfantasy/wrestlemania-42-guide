What to Watch This Week

Can't watch everything? Here are the most important episodes to catch before WrestleMania.

📺

Monday Night Raw

Mondays at 8 PM ET on Netflix. CM Punk, Rhea Ripley, Liv Morgan, Stephanie Vaquer, and Oba Femi's storylines play out here. The go-home show (last Raw before WrestleMania) is the most important!

📺

Friday Night SmackDown

Fridays at 8 PM ET on USA Network. Cody Rhodes, Randy Orton, Roman Reigns, and Jade Cargill's stories happen here. Watch the SmackDown before WrestleMania for final drama.

🌟

The Go-Home Shows

The final Raw and SmackDown before WrestleMania are the most dramatic episodes of the year -- final confrontations, contract signings, and last attacks. DON'T miss these!
